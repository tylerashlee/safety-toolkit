Cybersecurity Safety Checklist 🔐

Use this checklist to ensure you're staying secure every day:

  • 🔑 Change your passwords immediately if there is a breach.
  • 📲 Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) wherever possible. This makes it harder for hackers to gain unauthorized access, even if they have your password.
  • 💻 Keep your operating system and apps up to date. Updates patch critical security holes that hackers exploit!
  • 🛡️ Only share personal info on trusted, secure websites. Check for typos in the URL and look for the HTTPS or padlock symbol to know if it's secure.
  • 🔒 Use strong, unique passwords for each account. This ensures that if one password is breached, your other accounts remain secure!
  • 🛡️ Regularly back up your important data to the cloud and external drives.
  • 🕵️ Be cautious about phishing attempts and scam emails. To learn more about how to spot phishing you can visit PhishingQuiz.WithGoogle.com
